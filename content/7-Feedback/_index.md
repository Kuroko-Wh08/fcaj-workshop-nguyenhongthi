# Sharing and Feedback

## 1. Personal reflections

- The working environment was comfortable and open. The workspace was cool and the Wi-Fi connection was stable.
- The Admin team was friendly, enthusiastic, and dedicated when supporting students. Although communication was sometimes strict, I could still see their sense of responsibility and commitment.
- Joining the FCAJ Bootcamp 2026 was the right decision. Although cloud computing was a new environment and differed from parts of my major, I had the opportunity to apply what I had learned and directly configure and deploy a complete website on AWS infrastructure.
- The company provided desks, chairs, Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and power outlets, creating a convenient and comfortable working environment.
- Saturday Meetup sessions helped me learn about additional topics and gain new practical insights.

## 2. Feedback

I hope the program can distribute on-site working sessions more evenly, particularly for participants who currently have fewer opportunities to work at the office.
